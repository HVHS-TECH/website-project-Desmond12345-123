Park Gallery

A photographic tour through Wellington's five finest green spaces.

01
Bush Walk

Khandallah Park

Dense native bush and winding trails tucked above the suburb of Khandallah. Home to tūī, kererū and stunning harbour views.

© Photo: Khandallah.png — All rights reserved. Used for educational purposes only.

01
Pool & Trails

Khandallah Park — Pool & Walks

Khandallah Park offers both a community pool and a network of bush trails — a rare combination that makes it a year-round destination for the whole family.

© Photos: Khandallah Pool.png, Khandallah.png — All rights reserved. Used for educational purposes only.

02
Regional

Belmont Regional Park

Vast open ridgelines stretching from the Hutt Valley to the Porirua Harbour. Wild, remote-feeling and just minutes from the suburbs.

© Photo: Belmont.png — All rights reserved. Used for educational purposes only.

02
Trails

Belmont — On the Trail

Belmont's trail network winds through native vegetation and open farmland, welcoming mountain bikers, horse riders and trampers alike.

© Photo: Belmont2.png — All rights reserved. Used for educational purposes only.

03
Community

Kilbirnie Park

The heartbeat of Wellington's eastern suburbs. Spacious sports fields, a busy skate park and a newly upgraded playground — there's always something on at Kilbirnie.

© Photos: Kilbirnie.png, Kilbirnie2.jfif — All rights reserved. Used for educational purposes only.

04
Landmark

Wellington Botanic Gardens

Twenty-six hectares of hillside sanctuary. From the formal rose garden to the native bush reserve — a world-class garden in the heart of the city.

© Photo: Botanic.png — All rights reserved. Used for educational purposes only.

04
Rose Garden

Botanic Gardens — In Bloom

Every season reveals a new face of the Botanic Gardens. The rose garden is breathtaking in summer, while the autumn foliage rivals any garden in the country.

© Photo: Botanic2.png — All rights reserved. Used for educational purposes only.

05
Family

Avalon Park

Lower Hutt's beloved family park. Shaded lawns, the iconic miniature train ride, BBQ areas and the gentle sounds of the Hutt River in the background — a perfect Wellington afternoon.

© Photos: Avalon.png, Avalon2.png — All rights reserved. Used for educational purposes only.
